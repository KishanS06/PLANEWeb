PLANE focuses on general health awareness promotion, social security, economic empowerment for teenage mothers, and preventing violence against children, human rights promotion and mitigating the impact of violence against children and the youth and other related interventions. PLANE implements programs aimed at educational support to orphans and vulnerable children through provision of scholastic materials and school dues. It also implements programs for empowering the young mothers and rural women through livelihood interventions and it greatly supports interventions focusing at gender-based violence prevention, sexual reproductive health rights, water, sanitation and hygiene for rural communities.
This website and branch of the organization is dedicated to the outreach of Platform for the Needy to the United States of America. Promoters include adults and students across the nation, with the Ugandan coordinater being Mr.Wadell Steven. Through a branch of the organization, there can be an increased spread of awareness, and an outlet that can provide funding (more in the donation section below) and a chance to expand the organization. Furthermore, operations are being taken to spread the organization through contacting local pastors across the nation for a chance of support for the underpriveleged individuals in Uganda. Since individuals in the region of Uganda are susceptible to many genetically-based disorders and diseases, there is increased prevalence of stunted children and strains of cancer, which requires interventions that derive from international fronts.
